📱 YOUR PROBLEM (from screenshot):
   Google Play Protect is blocking HisabApp installation
   Message: "This app can request access to sensitive data"

✅ QUICK SOLUTION (2 minutes):

   Step 1: Open Settings on your phone
   
   Step 2: Search for "Play Protect" in settings
           (or go to Google → Security → Google Play Protect)
   
   Step 3: Tap the ⚙️ (Settings) icon in top right corner
   
   Step 4: Turn OFF "Scan apps with Play Protect"
           (toggle should turn gray)
   
   Step 5: Go back and install app-release.apk
           No more blocking! ✅
   
   Step 6: (Optional) Turn Play Protect back ON after install
           Already installed apps won't be affected

🔍 WHY IS THIS HAPPENING?

   ❌ Your app requests SMS permission (READ_SMS, RECEIVE_SMS)
   ❌ APK is unsigned (development build)  
   ❌ Not from Play Store (unknown developer)
   ❌ Google doesn't recognize this app

   ✅ YOUR APP IS SAFE! No virus, no malware!
   ✅ SMS permission is for reading bank transactions only
   ✅ All data stays on your phone, never sent to any server

🎯 ALTERNATIVE METHODS:

   ────────────────────────────────────
   1. Enable USB debugging (Method 2, step 1-2)
